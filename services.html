Amharic Autism Aid app

This application is developed to help people with autism which provides sort of things that are cataloged in home page. Under the catalogue, thing which gives awareness about autism, frequently used words to learn, name of transportation options, months of the year, wears and so on have been organized in picture and sound format Hence, when people with Autism click the picture in the catalogue they can express their feelings, learn new words. Besides, The developed application enables families of autistic individuals to contact the Autism Center for support and consultation.
